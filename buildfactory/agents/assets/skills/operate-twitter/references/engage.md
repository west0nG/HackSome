# Engage in public

Use this playbook for public mentions, relevant conversations, replies, quote
posts, likes, follows, and account discovery. Do not enter Direct Messages.

## Find the right context

Start from the Goal and company state, not an endless home feed. Depending on
the result, inspect public mentions, a target account or list, a focused search,
or the exact conversation already recorded in company state.

Read the full post and enough of the conversation to understand what the author
actually said, who is involved, and whether the moment is still current. A
keyword match alone is not context.

## Choose a meaningful public action

Prefer actions that advance a real relationship, answer a concrete question,
surface useful evidence, or teach the company something about its audience.

- Reply when a direct contribution belongs in the existing conversation.
- Quote when the company's added context is independently useful to its own
  audience.
- Like when it truthfully marks a useful signal or relationship touch, not to
  manufacture a metric.
- Follow when future posts from that account are genuinely relevant, not to
  trigger a reciprocal follow.
- Observe or do nothing when the company cannot add value.

There is no daily interaction quota, top-ten requirement, or rule that every
mention deserves an answer. A small relevant question can be more valuable
than a high-reach unrelated post.

## Act and verify

Reconfirm the current company account and exact target immediately before the
action. For a reply or quote, run `de-ai-ify` on final public copy. Afterward,
open the conversation or target profile again and confirm the reply, quote,
like, or follow state is actually visible.

Record durable relationship context, a promise, useful language, or a result
only when it will change a later decision. Do not turn every like or follow
into a company-memory event log.
