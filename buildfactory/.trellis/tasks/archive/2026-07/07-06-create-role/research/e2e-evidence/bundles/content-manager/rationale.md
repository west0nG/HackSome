We should have a content manager role. Content is very important for a modern
company and none of our current roles is specifically called "content manager".
Having a dedicated content manager would improve our content quality, keep our
messaging consistent, and free up the other departments to focus on their core
strengths. This role would collaborate closely with growth and builder to make
sure our content is always excellent and on-brand. It seems like it would be
very useful for many kinds of future work.
